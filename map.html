Current Reports in Toronto



The CitySafe Map is a Map that can easily tell its user how safe an area of Toronto is by having a heat map of colours based on the severity the crimes committed.

It has four main colours indicating level of severity:

Green: Areas indicated in Green have little to no incident reports reported in the area

Yellow: Areas indicated in Yellow have some incident reports reported in the area

Orange: Areas indicated in Orange have a considerable amount of incidents reported

Red: Areas indicated in Red have many incidents reported
